Breakfast of Champions

How to Get the Most Out of Mornings

Breakfast can be a tricky meal when you're trying to stay healthy. The allure of waffles and bacon can be strong, but there are some alternatives out there which can satisfy just as well.

  • Substitute bacon with chicken sausage or smoked salmon to lower calories and add nutrients
  • Replace waffles or pancakes with sweet potato or hash browns
  • Use olive oil instead of butter to fry eggs

Often cooking just after waking is not an appealing task. Sticking a pop tart in the toaster is a lot easier than cooking a healthy meal, especially when we haven't had our coffee or tea yet. This is why making a big batch of breakfast foods earlier can keep us from the sugery treats. Just stick a tupperware in the microwave and we've got ourselves a healthy breakfast!
